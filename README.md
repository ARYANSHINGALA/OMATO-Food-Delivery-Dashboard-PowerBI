🍔 OMATO Food Delivery Dashboard (Power BI)
By Aryan Shingala

🧩 Project Overview

The OMATO Food Delivery Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze food delivery business performance.
It provides insights into order quantity, transactions, customer behavior, payment methods, and food preferences.

This project demonstrates strong capabilities in data visualization, business intelligence, and dashboard development using Power BI.

🎯 Project Objective

The dashboard is built to help stakeholders:

Track total orders, transactions, and average quantity ,
Analyze monthly and category-wise order trends , 
Understand customer ordering behavior by member type ,
Identify top-performing food items ,
Evaluate payment method usage ,
Monitor delivery success vs cancellation rate ,

⚙️ Tech Stack

Component	Tool / Technology = 
Data Visualization : Power BI ,
Data Cleaning	: Power Query ,
Data Modeling	: Power BI Data Model ,
DAX Calculations : Measures & Calculated Columns ,
Visual Elements :	KPI Cards, Bar Charts, Line Charts, Donut Charts, Tables ,
Interactivity	: Slicers & Filters

📂 Data Source

Dataset Type: Food delivery transactional dataset ,
Format: Excel / CSV

Key Data Fields:

Order ID ,
Order Date ,
Quantity ,
Transactions ,
Member Type (Gold, Regular) ,
Food Type ,
Payment Mode (UPI, COD, Card) ,
Delivery Status (Delivered / Cancelled),
Restaurant Type.

🔄 Data Processing

Removed duplicates and missing values ,
Standardized date and numeric formats ,
Built calculated columns and measures using DAX ,
Created relationships between tables ,
Applied filters and slicers for dynamic analysis.

🌟 Key Dashboard Features

🔹 KPI Cards

Total Quantity: 15.10K ,
Total Transactions: 3K ,
Average Quantity: 6.

🔹 Sales & Order Trends

Monthly Quantity Trend ,
Transactions Trend by Month & Year ,
Daily/Time-based analysis.

🔹 Category & Food Insights

Top 10 Food Items by Quantity,
Quantity by Food Category (Appetizer, Main Course, Snack, etc.),
Member Type Analysis (Gold vs Regular).

🔹 Customer & Payment Insights

Payment Methods Distribution (UPI, COD, Card) ,
Delivery Status (Delivered vs Cancelled) ,
Customer segmentation insights.

🔹 Interactive Filters

Month Filter ,
Member Type Filter ,
Payment Mode Filter ,
Food Type Filter , 
Restaurant Type Filter.

🔍 Key Business Insights

Top Selling Item: Samosa (highest quantity ordered) ,
Major Revenue Driver: Main Course category dominates orders , 
Preferred Payment Method: UPI is the most used payment method ,
Delivery Success Rate: ~95% orders successfully delivered ,
Customer Type Insight: Gold members contribute significantly to total orders ,
Trend Insight: Orders show fluctuation with peak months in Feb & March.
